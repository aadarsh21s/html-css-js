Follow The Step-By-Step Instruction:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C).
  2. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating after each; stir in vanilla.
  4. Whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  9. Let cookies cool on the sheets for a few minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  10. Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies!

Notes

Store in an airtight container for up to a week or freeze for longer storage.

Variations

Add nuts, dried fruit, or swap half the chips for white chocolate.

Tips

  • Chill dough for 30 minutes before baking for chewier cookies.
  • Use a cookie scoop for uniform sizes.
  • Try dark, milk, or flavored chocolate chips.

Serving Suggestions

Perfect with cold milk or coffee; great as a gift in a tin.

Nutrition Information

About 150 calories per cookie: 7 g fat, 20 g carbs, 2 g protein (approx.).
